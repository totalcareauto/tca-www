About

Total Care Auto, based in Salt Lake City, Utah, has been providing uncompromising service and coverage for our customers for over 30 years. Our reputation and products have truly proven themselves over time. Total Care Auto is the comprehensive solution to protecting and maintaining your second biggest investment next to your house, your vehicle.

Originally offered only through our dealerships in the Western United States, we are now providing these products to other dealership groups and directly to consumers.

Automotive dealerships interested in carrying our products can contact us directly using the information on this site.

Consumers interested in our services can do the same, or you can contact your local dealer directly and ask about our complete line of Total Care Auto products.

We look forward to hearing from you and thank you for letting us keep your vehicle looking and running showroom new.
